About Us

About Maati Impact

Maati means soil. It is the foundation things grow from. That philosophy drives everything we do — building sustainability strategies that are grounded, practical, and designed to last.

We are a boutique consulting firm helping organisations use sustainability as a driver of better business, products, and long-term growth. We identify gaps and risks, evaluate programmes and products, and design practical, high-impact strategies that inform real business decisions.

We believe sustainability should sit at the heart of business strategy — not as a compliance exercise, but as a lever for efficiency, innovation, and growth.

Our policy research and development work complements our sustainability offerings — enabling credible frameworks, informed decision-making, and long-term impact across your organisation's internal policies, supply chains, and operations.
